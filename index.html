What did I learn?

Prefixes

These are the special beginnings we add to words.

● "re-" means to do something again
    👉 Replay, Redo, Rewrite
● "pre-" means before or earlier than
    👉 Prepare, Preview, Preschool
● "mis-" means wrongly or badly
    👉 Mistake, Misplace, Misspell

Suffixes

These are the special endings we add to words.

● "-ful" means full of or having a lot of
    👉 Colourful, Helpful, Playful
● "-less" means without or not having
    👉 Fearless, Endless, Harmless

💡 Remember, prefixes and suffixes are like magic spells that change words. You can use them to create all sorts of new and exciting words!
